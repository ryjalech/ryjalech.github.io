About me

I'm a professionally trained editor with diplomatic communications style and strong analytical eye. I have over twelve years of experience strategizing and developing internal communications, technical guides, newsletters, and web publications — with experience in training/instruction, project/program management, process development and continuous improvement, and team management. I love documenting complex processes and technical systems and in easily digestible content with clarity, context, and rationale.

To me editing is like photo processing. I start with a plan to define the subject and approach, refine the framing and exposure, and then adjust the sliders until the piece best expresses its subject. Editing text has some different nuances, but the core mentality is the same: a document draft is like a raw image; I let that raw content tell me what it is and what it needs, and I work to bring that out.
